Before You Get Your Puppy



If you do not know how to raise and train a puppy, he/she will most surely develop a number of behavior, temperament, and training problems. All these problems are easily preventable, IF you educate yourself BEFORE YOU GET YOUR PUPPY!

In an ongoing attempt to educate prospective puppy owners, Dr. Ian Dunbar, has made his book "Before You Get Your Puppy", available online for FREE. Click here: "Before You Get Your Puppy" for a free download. This book is one of our most highly recommended books.

If you would like some assistance in researching which type of pup best fits your lifestyle, or in finding or choosing a pup, please call or email us! We would be happy to help in any way we can!

Without a doubt, the biggest reason adolescent dogs are surrendered to shelters or rescue groups, is people making the wrong choice of which pup fits their lifestyle, followed by lack of training and prevention of behavior problems. Don't let this sad scenario happen to you!!
